Lead Capture Widget

A beautiful, conversion-optimized lead capture form for emphxs.com

Modal Popup Demo

Click the button below to open the lead capture form in a modal popup. This is perfect for "Get a Quote" buttons.

Integration Code:

<!-- Include the widget script (place before closing </body> tag) --> <script src="https://YOUR-DOMAIN.com/js/lead-capture-widget.js" data-api-url="https://YOUR-API-DOMAIN.com"></script> <!-- Add a button to trigger the modal --> <button onclick="EmphxsLeadWidget.openModal()">Get a Free Quote</button>

Inline Form Demo

The form can also be embedded directly into your page. Perfect for contact pages or landing pages.

Integration Code:

<!-- Add a container where you want the form --> <div id="emphxs-lead-form"></div> <!-- Include the widget script --> <script src="https://YOUR-DOMAIN.com/js/lead-capture-widget.js" data-api-url="https://YOUR-API-DOMAIN.com"></script>
